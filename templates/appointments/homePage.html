About Us

DocConnect is a unified hospital appointment management platform designed to seamlessly integrate multiple hospitals into a centralized system. Whether you're a patient looking to book a consultation or a hospital managing schedules, DocConnect simplifies the process with intelligent doctor discovery, real-time slot availability, and secure appointment handling—all under one interface.

Our Services

  • Multi-Hospital Integration: Connects patients with doctors across different hospitals through a single platform.
  • Doctor Discovery: Search for specialists by department or expertise and view availability in real time.
  • Smart Appointment Booking: Schedule, view, and manage appointments easily with secure slot reservation.
  • Cross-Hospital Data Mapping: Advanced schema-matching algorithms enable consistent access to diverse hospital databases.
  • Patient Dashboard: Personalized space for patients to review past bookings, upcoming appointments, and manage profiles.
